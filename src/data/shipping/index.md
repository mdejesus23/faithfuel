## How Ordering Works

After you place an order, our team will contact you directly to arrange the details. This personal approach helps us ensure your order is handled with care.

## Shipping Details

Once your order is submitted, we will reach out to you to:

- Confirm your shipping address
- Discuss payment options
- Calculate shipping fees (if applicable)
- Provide an estimated delivery timeline

Shipping fees vary depending on your location and will be communicated before finalizing your order.

## Payment

Payment details will be arranged when we contact you after your order is placed. We'll work with you to find a convenient payment method.

## Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be happy with your purchase. If there's an issue with your order:

- **Damaged or defective items** – Contact us and we'll arrange a replacement or refund
- **Wrong size or item** – Let us know and we'll work out an exchange
- **Change of mind** – Please reach out within 7 days of receiving your order

To request a return or exchange, simply contact us. We'll provide instructions on how to proceed, including any return shipping details.

## Contact Us

Have questions about shipping or returns? Reach out through our contact page and we'll be happy to help.
