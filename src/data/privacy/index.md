# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 25, 2026

## About This Site

FaithFuel is a small fundraising project. We collect only the information needed to process and deliver your orders.

## What We Collect

When you place an order, we collect:

- Your name
- Email address
- Contact number
- Order details (items, quantities)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Contact you about your order to collect payments, confirm your shipping address and fulfill your order
- Keep records of orders for our fundraising

## Where Your Data Is Stored

Your order information is stored securely in Google Sheets, which we use to manage and track orders. Google's privacy practices apply to this storage.

## What We Don't Do

- We don't sell your information
- We don't use your data for marketing
- We don't share your information with third parties (except for order fulfillment)

## Your Cart

We store your shopping cart in your browser's local storage. This stays on your device and is cleared when you complete your order or clear your browser data.

## Your Rights

You can request to:

- See what information we have about you
- Have your information deleted

Just contact us and we'll take care of it.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about your privacy or your data, reach out to us through our contact page.

_This is a small fundraising project run by real people, not a big corporation. We respect your privacy and handle your information with care._
