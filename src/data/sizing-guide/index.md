## T-Shirt Size Chart

Our t-shirts follow standard unisex sizing. Use the chart below to find your best fit.

| Size | Chest (inches) | Length (inches) |
| ---- | -------------- | --------------- |
| XS   | 32 - 34        | 26              |
| S    | 35 - 37        | 27              |
| M    | 38 - 40        | 28              |
| L    | 41 - 43        | 29              |
| XL   | 44 - 46        | 30              |
| XXL  | 47 - 49        | 31              |

## How to Measure

### Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal.

### Length

Measure from the highest point of your shoulder down to your preferred hem length.

## Fit Tips

- **Prefer a relaxed fit?** Go one size up
- **Like it fitted?** Stick with your measured size
- **Between sizes?** We recommend sizing up for comfort

## Not Sure?

If you're unsure about sizing, feel free to reach out through our contact page. We're happy to help you find the right fit!
